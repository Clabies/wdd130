Overview

Purpose

We are a rafting company that offers unique and fun experiences on the river, encouraging contact with nature, teamwork and overcoming challenges. Our mission is to provide a quality service, safety and professionalism that meets the expectations of our customers and contributes to the sustainable development of local communities.

Audience

Who are the target customers?
The company's target audience are people who want to live a different adventure, have fun and enjoy nature.

What do they want?
They are looking for information about routes, prices, safety measures, testimonials and company contact details.

How will my audience usually access my site?
Audience will access through mobile devices, such as smartphones or tablets and from desktop computers.
