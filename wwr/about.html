Turbulent Adventure

We are a rafting company that offers unique and fun experiences on the river, encouraging contact with nature, teamwork and overcoming challenges. Our mission is to provide a quality service, safety and professionalism that meets the expectations of our customers and contributes to the sustainable development of local communities.

History

Arturo and Claudia are two friends who shared a passion for canoeing. Since they were kids, they had practiced this sport in the river that passed near their hometown, in the state of Colorado. Eventually, they became experts and decided to open their own canoeing company, called Turbulent Adventure.

Turbulent Adventure offers guided tours on different stretches of the river, adapted to the level and preferences of the clients. The tours vary in length, difficulty and scenery. We offer quiet tours suitable for beginners or families with children, where you could appreciate the natural beauty of the environment and observe the river's fauna and flora. Others are more exciting and challenging, where rapids, rocks and currents must be overcome, testing the skill and adrenaline of the participants.
